About Me

Empowering Voices, Building Leaders

I'm Aafreen Naaz, the President of Toastmasters International and L&D Trainer through my initiative @SpeakUptoStandOut, based in Bengaluru, India.


With over 9 years of experience across top organizations like Cargill, Infosys BPM, and Genpact, I specialize in finance (P2P, O2C, R2R) using SAP ERP, while also dedicating myself to leadership and communication development.


I help individuals develop powerful communication skills, master the art of presentation, and build leadership capabilities. My sessions are practical, engaging, and designed to help you find your voice.


I hold an M.Com in Accounting & Finance and a Bachelor of Laws (LLB), bringing a unique cross-disciplinary perspective.


Let's work together to unlock your full potential as a speaker and leader.
